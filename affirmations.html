Daily Affirmations

For Strength

I have the strength to overcome any challenge.

My feelings are valid, and I am allowed to feel them without judgment.

For Self-Worth

I am enough, just as I am, in this exact moment.

I do not need to prove my worth because of someone else.

For Calm

I am letting go of what I cannot control.

I choose to focus on what I can control and let go of what I cannot.

"Take a moment to repeat these to yourself, and believe them."
