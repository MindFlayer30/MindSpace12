Take one deep breath and begin when you are ready.

Sit with a straight back or lie down, ensuring muscles are relaxed.

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 4 seconds, and hold for 4 seconds. Repeat for several minutes to regulate oxygen and reduce stress.

Breathe in for 3 seconds, hold for 3 seconds, and exhale for 3 seconds to quickly manage acute anxiety.
